Who we are and
what we do

Making the best milk tea, coffee and
lemon fusion in town...

BobaTime Food Corner began with a simple love for milk tea. We enjoyed it so much and began to feel that we could make it even better and share it with our fellow Olongapeños and with the rest of our community.

On the 10th of June 2019, we opened our shop and launched our signature drinks, prime milk teas, cheese series and lemon fusions.

In our first year in May 2020, the pandemic challenged us to be bold, and go beyond our boundaries. So we initiated the milk tea party concept in Olongapo City which caters to exclusive family gatherings providing safe and delicious beverages from the comfort of their homes.
